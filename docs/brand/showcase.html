1. The Brand Identity

The Watch Alley PH is a precision bench workshop and private collecting desk for dedicated Filipino watch collectors. The brand logo leverages the **drafting compass** or **watchmaker's inspection caliper** to form the letters inside the wordmark—symbolizing rigorous calibration, honest written disclosures, and horological expertise.
